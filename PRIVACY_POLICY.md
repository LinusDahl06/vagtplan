# Privacy Policy for Vagtplan

**Last Updated: December 21, 2024**

## Introduction

Lincware ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Vagtplan mobile application (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:
- **Name**: Your full name for profile identification
- **Username**: A unique username for your account
- **Email Address**: For account authentication and communication
- **Password**: Encrypted and stored securely via Firebase Authentication
- **Profile Picture**: Optional photo you choose to upload

### Workspace Data

When you use the Service, we collect and store:
- **Workspace Information**: Names of workspaces you create or join
- **Schedule Data**: Shift schedules, dates, and hours worked
- **Employee Data**: Names, usernames, and roles of employees in your workspaces
- **Role and Permission Settings**: Custom roles and permissions you configure
- **Shift Presets**: Custom shift configurations you create

### Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service:
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used
- **Log Data**: Error logs, crash reports via Firebase
- **Authentication Data**: Login timestamps, authentication tokens

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:
- **To Provide the Service**: Create and manage your account, workspaces, and schedules
- **To Improve the Service**: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
- **To Communicate**: Send important service updates, security alerts
- **To Ensure Security**: Detect and prevent unauthorized access, fraud, and abuse
- **To Comply with Legal Obligations**: Respond to legal requests, enforce our terms

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services:
- **Firestore Database**: All workspace, schedule, and user data
- **Firebase Authentication**: User credentials and authentication tokens
- **Firebase Storage**: Profile pictures and uploaded images
- **Location**: Data centers operated by Google Cloud Platform

### Security Measures

We implement security measures including:
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Firebase Security Rules**: Server-side access control preventing unauthorized access
- **Authentication**: Secure Firebase Authentication with password hashing
- **Input Validation**: Client and server-side validation to prevent malicious data
- **Limited Access**: Role-based permissions controlling who can access what data

### Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy:
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data deleted when you delete your account
- **Backups**: Backup data retained for up to 30 days
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
